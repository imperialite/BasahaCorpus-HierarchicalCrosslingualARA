Kon wala si Nanay


Tandi Jackson




Michael Mape





Si Bubu naga istar sa isa ka uma. Nagamasakit siya. Si mama niya nagapangita sa iya.





Sin o maninlo sang balay? Sin o maobra sa talamnan?





Sin o maluto sang pagkaon? Sin o manglaba sang mga bayo?





Maobra ako sa talamnan.





Maninlo ako sang amun blay.





Maluto ako sang amun pagkaon





Manglaba ako sang mga bayo





Sin o mabantay sng bata?





Kami tanan mabantay sang bata.





Comprehension Questions

1. Where is Mama going on the bus? Why?

2. Is the family happy to see mom go? How do you know?

3. Why do you think dad looks worried?

4. Who helps dad?

5. How does the boy help in the garden?

6. How does the girl help clean the house?

7. Who does the cooking?

8. Who looks after the baby?

9. Will mom be pleased when she gets home? Why?

10. What things do you do to help at home?

Activity

Draw things that you do to help at home.
